About Us

Mcmarry College of Advanced Studies is a certified A’Level training institution dedicated to preparing students for direct university admission through IJMB and JUPEB programs.

Our mission is to make higher education more accessible by eliminating the barriers many students face with JAMB.

Our Programs

IJMB

  • Affiliated with ABU, Zaria
  • Duration: 9–12 months
  • Admission into 200 Level without JAMB
  • Recognized by 70+ universities

JUPEB

  • Established by UNILAG
  • Duration: 9–12 months
  • Admission into 200 Level without JAMB
  • Recognized by 60+ universities

How to Apply

  1. Fill the Online Application Form
  2. Choose your preferred program (IJMB or JUPEB)
  3. Pay your registration fee
  4. Begin your journey to 200 Level

Note: You must have WAEC, NECO, or NABTEB (or be awaiting results).
