About Us

Mcmarry College of Advanced Studies is a certified A’Level training institution dedicated to preparing students for direct university admission through IJMB and JUPEB programs. We offer expert-led tutorials, a structured curriculum, and full support for admission into over 70 universities in Nigeria via Direct Entry (DE).

Our mission is to make higher education more accessible by eliminating the barriers many students face with JAMB.
